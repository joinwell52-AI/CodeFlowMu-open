# Architect Decision Record Review

Use this playbook to prepare or review architecture decision notes without automatically adopting ADRs.

## Output

```text
# Architecture Decision Note

## 1. Decision Candidate
What decision is being proposed.

## 2. Context
Why the decision is needed now.

## 3. Options

| Option | Pros | Cons | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|

## 4. Recommendation
Recommended option and reason.

## 5. Required Authority
Who must approve: PM / ADMIN / protocol governance.

## 6. Non-adoption Statement
This note is not an adopted ADR until accepted through the required governance path.
```

## FCoP Boundaries

- Do not auto-create or auto-adopt ADRs.
- Do not add adopted-pending `0003` from this playbook.
- Do not rewrite protocol docs from a decision note.
